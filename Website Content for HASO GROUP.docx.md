### **About Us**

**Who We Are:**  
Health Affairs for Youth Initiatives (**HAYi**) is the **social impact arm of HASO GROUP**, committed to advancing **youth health, gender equity, and empowerment**. Through evidence-based programs, community engagement, and youth-led innovation, HAYi ensures all young people have access to the **knowledge, services, and opportunities** they need to thrive.

**Mission:**  
To promote **inclusive youth health and well-being** through education, advocacy, innovation, and community-driven programs, ensuring that all young people—regardless of gender, background, or ability—have equitable access to resources to thrive.

**Vision:**  
A Rwanda where **all young people are healthy, empowered, and socially included**, with access to quality health services, supportive environments, and economic opportunities.

**Values:**

* Inclusivity  
* Integrity  
* Empowerment  
* Innovation  
* Collaboration

### **Programs**

**Overview Text:**  
HAYi implements targeted programs that address **sexual and reproductive health, mental health, healthy lifestyles, GBV prevention, digital innovation, leadership, and socio-economic empowerment**.

**Featured Program – Menya, Wirinde, Ubeho:**

* **Description:** A school-based initiative empowering learners with **accurate SRHR knowledge** to make safe and informed choices.  
* **Target Group:** Students in secondary schools and out-of-school youth.  
* **Locations:** Gatsibo, Karongi, Nyarugenge  
* **Duration:** 6 months  
* **Key Activities:** Peer educator training, SRHR club strengthening, expert-led sessions, monitoring and evaluation, endline reporting.  
* **Visuals / CTA:** “Learn More” → Project Detail Page
